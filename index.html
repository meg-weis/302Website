About Me

I am a Digital Culture and Design major with a minor in Film and Production studies. I am a Junior at Coastal Carolina University. I like colorful abstract art, but also I appreciate simpler pieces that have just a few colors. I love movies, and the way that movies are made fascinates me. I also love animals and space. What first got me into this type of thing was the love of mystery and how shows and movies created mystical powers and superpowers. Seeing this type of thing on screen made me wonder how people created effects and creatures that are not human but are creatures like Dobby from Harry Potter and Groot from Guardians of the Galaxy. Also, seeing behind the scenes of different shows and seeing how a magical world started from a green screen.

For my career, I would love to go into animation of visual effects. I have always found it cool to watch all of the end credits and see all the different people involved in making the movie. You learn about the actors, doctors, editors, and a few other people. But you donâ€™t hear a lot about the people changing a green screen into a beautiful backdrop for a movie. Now that I have seen credits for many films, I always said to myself that I would be a name on that screen even if no one stays to watch the credits. I like to be creative, and I think this is an excellent way to do that.

My aesthetic is a bit all over the place because I like the look of many different pieces of art and the ways things are made. I like bright, colorful things, but I also like the look of black and white art. I prefer pictures of animals or things better than some with people in them. Abstract things have always caught my eye more than the art of something, and it is also the type of art that I like doing the most. I love abstract because of how each person can see something else in the piece of art.
